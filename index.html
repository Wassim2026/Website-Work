Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) and Pandemics

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is a growing global crisis, claiming over 1.2 million lives annually, while future pandemics threaten global health security. Without coordinated action, AMR could cause 10 million deaths per year by 2050, and pandemics could devastate economies and healthcare systems.

IFNAP, born from the XpertOne Health Conference 2025, unites experts to address these urgent challenges. We connect scientists, policymakers, healthcare professionals, and industry leaders to share knowledge, innovate solutions, and build resilient health systems. Networking is our strength—together, we can prevent, prepare for, and respond to AMR and pandemics effectively.

Your Hub for Networking, Collaboration, and Action!

Are you passionate about tackling AMR and preparing for pandemics? IFNAP offers a global platform to connect, collaborate, and act. Whether you’re a researcher, clinician, policymaker, or advocate, IFNAP provides the tools, events, and community to make an impact.

We are dedicated to fostering a connected network that drives solutions to AMR and pandemic threats. Join us to share expertise, access resources, and collaborate on initiatives that protect global health.
